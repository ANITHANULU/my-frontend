The rose is a type of flower that is known for its beauty and lovely scent. It comes in many colors like red, pink, white, yellow, and even blue or black. People around the world love roses. They are often given as gifts on special days like Valentine’s Day or Mother’s Day to show love or thanks. Roses have a special part in their middle called petals, which are soft and can be used to make perfume or rose water because they smell so good. The bush where roses grow can be small or big, with thorns on its stems to protect it from animals that might eat it. Gardening fans enjoy growing roses and take care of them by giving them water, sunlight, and cutting off dead parts so new flowers can bloom.
